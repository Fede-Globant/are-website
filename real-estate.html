Real Estate

Enabling the World's Most Innovative Companies to Develop Scientific & Technological Breakthroughs to Advance Human Health

Our Essential Labspace® Real Estate

Alexandria’s highly sophisticated real estate platform enables our tenants to focus on COVID-19-related and other mission-critical diagnostics, testing, therapies, and vaccines. Even in the height of the global pandemic, we have maintained 24/7 operations across all our regions given the essential nature of our real estate and the life-changing research being done in our buildings.  

We are uniquely focused on cultivating life science, technology, and agtech campuses that provide our innovative tenants with highly dynamic, collaborative, and intentionally designed environments that enhance their ability to successfully recruit and retain world-class talent and inspire productivity, efficiency, creativity, and success.
