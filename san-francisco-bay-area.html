San Francisco Bay Area

Approximately 8.5M RSF

Alexandria Center for Science and Technology®

The San Francisco Bay Area is the historical birthplace of the biotechnology industry. Alexandria is the dominant market leader in providing first-class office/laboratory space to the life science and technology industry in San Francisco. The Alexandria Center for Science and Technology® in Mission Bay is a dynamic and customized urban campus designed to foster business collaboration and innovation among its tenants with immediate proximity to the renowned University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). San Francisco is anchored by world-class biopharmaceutical and high-technology companies, and is a leader in the nation in attracting life science and technology venture funding.
