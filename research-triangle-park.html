Research Triangle

Approximately 3.9M RSF

The Research Triangle has a long tradition of life science and agtech innovation. Home to the largest assemblage of leading industry crop protection and seed companies, renowned academic institutions – Duke University, the University of North Carolina, and North Carolina State University, skilled scientific and managerial talent, and an expanding pool of ag-focused venture capital,  the Research Triangle has emerged as the country's most important, dense, and diverse agtech cluster.
