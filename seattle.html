Seattle

Approximately 1.6M RSF

Seattle has become the location of choice for innovative companies, drawing on water views, easy freeway access, excellent public transportation, and an abundance of employee amenities. With nearly one million square feet of Class A office/laboratory space primarily clustered in South Lake Union, Alexandria is well situated to continue to meet the needs of the life science community within the current portfolio.
