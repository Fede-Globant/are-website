Venture Investments

Supporting Companies to Transform the Lives of Patients

Founded in 1996, Alexandria Venture Investments is the company's strategic venture capital arm. Alexandria Venture Investments provides long-term strategic investment capital to innovative life science, technology, and agtech entities developing breakthrough technologies and therapies. 

Leveraging our experience and in-depth understanding of the life science, technology, and agtech industries, our long-term relationships with leading investors, and our world-class scientific advisory network, we are uniquely positioned to fund seed-, early-, and growth-stage companies.


Areas of Focus

  • Biopharma
  • Diagnostics
  • Research Tools
  • AgTech
  • Technology





Track Record
