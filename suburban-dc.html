Maryland

Approximately 3.1M RSF

With facilities well situated along the I-270 Corridor (known as DNA Alley), Alexandria is a leading life science real estate provider in Maryland. A combination of federal agencies, leading academic institutions, and a large cluster of life science companies make this a strategic cluster location for Alexandria.
