Greater Boston

Approximately 8.6M RSF

Preeminent in the realm of scientific breakthroughs and innovative medical technologies, Greater Boston is the center of life science research and development in the eastern United States. Interaction between scientific researchers in the world's most prestigious academic institutions, medical practitioners in renowned teaching hospitals and entrepreneurs in public and private life science companies has spawned major advances in medicine and science, including dozens of Nobel-prize winning discoveries.
